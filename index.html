Ensuring universal internet access is essential for upholding human rights in the digital age. It fosters education, communication, and empowerment for all individuals, regardless of background or location.

Internet Access Statistics

Global Internet Penetration

Approximately 60% of the global population has access to the internet, leaving over 3.5 billion people without connectivity.

Digital Divide

The digital divide disproportionately affects marginalized communities, with rural areas and low-income households facing the greatest barriers to internet access.
